Based on the QRS complex, we can determine whether the Heart beat is coming from the Atrium or from the Ventricles.

This is an important piece of information, since we can determine which area of the heart will be needing treatment when an Arrhythmia is detected.

Atrial based arrhythmia's have a smaller QRS complex whereas Ventricular arrhythmia's have a longer QRS complex.
