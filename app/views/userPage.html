Arrythmia's

Based on the QRS complex, we can determine whether the Heart beat is coming from the Atrium or from the Ventricles.

This is an import piece of information, since we can determine which area of the heart will be needing treatment regarding a specific Arrhythmia.

Atrial based arrythmias have a smaller QRS complex whereas Ventricular Arrhythmias have a longer QRS complex.
